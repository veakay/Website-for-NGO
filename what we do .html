WHAT WE DO



WORKING METHODOLOGY

In order to build a growing community of leaders, Saksham has a model called ‘Attention for all’. In the short-term, through our program, we provide an opportunity to the brightest and most promising individuals to come forward and serve the society, being privileged with wisdom and knowledge to those who aren’t as privileged as us. Through this experience of teaching in slums and working with stakeholders of society like kids, volunteers (mostly UG students) we get exposed to the grassroots realities of India’s education system and begin to cultivate the knowledge, skills, and mindsets necessary to attain the aim that we have in our minds i.e building a larger movement for equity in education. Our working style follow 4:1 student to teacher ratio.All rights and decisions regarding the respective kids are being taken by respective volunteers only. Reason for this is we need to attain the goal of proper attention to all with an excellent provision of education.Volunteers just need to follow three major actions of keeping patience, tolerance and calmness.


OUR OTHER INITIATIVES

1. Evening initiative:
An initiative started for SSC students belonging to slums and government schools where our volunteers daily use to spend 2 hours with them to solve their queries.

2. Distribution drive:
Distribution of basic required study material to the students.

3. Independence Day Celebration:
Arranged some cultural activities, told them about the importance of the day, involved them in various activities like songs, dances and monologue, to identify the hidden talent of them.

4. Blood Donation Camp:
Blood Donation Camp has been organised to make them aware about importance of Blood Donation

5. Free Health Check-up:
A medical free health checkup camp has been arranged for those kids to diagnose them and to generate a health report for them

6. Antyoday-Children’s Marathon:
Marathon was being organised for them where we distributed them t-shirts, trackpant, caps free of cost and also arranged a carnival for them where volunteers arranged games for them, tatoo making stall for them.

7. Workshop on manners and ethics:
Volunteers conducted workshop for kids to make their personality, a well developed one with respect to manners, ethics and etiquettes.

8. Internship for volunteers:
Considering efforts of volunteers for the society, we decided to take care of their careers, we find and make sure that all pour volunteers should get desire internships with respect to their fields.

9. Placement Assistance:
As mentioned above, to take care of their careers, we arranged a free placement assistance from our alumni for the volunteers.

10. Psychological analysis of students:
We organised a special session with one of the certified human psychologist to analyse the students psychologically and sentimentally.
